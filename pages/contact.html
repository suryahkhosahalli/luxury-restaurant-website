Guest Assistance

Common Inquiries

While walk-in guests are accommodated based on dining room availability, table reservations are highly recommended to ensure you can experience our full seasonal menu. Valet parking and bar space are also prioritized for reservation guests.

We request a smart elegant attire in our main dining salons. Gentlemen are requested to wear collared shirts and closed-toed shoes. We kindly request guests avoid activewear, athletic sneakers, shorts, and flip-flops.

Yes, our culinary brigade is fully equipped to tailor courses for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and specific allergy profiles. We kindly ask that you note any severe dietary restrictions when booking your table or email us at least 24 hours prior to your visit.

Yes, complimentary valet parking is provided to all L'Étoile guests during our evening dinner services from Tuesday through Saturday. The valet stand is situated at our main entrance on Park Avenue starting at 5:00 PM.

We hold table bookings with a credit card guarantee. We require cancellations or modifications to be submitted at least 24 hours before your reservation time. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a charge of $50 per guest.
