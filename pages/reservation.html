Frequently Asked Questions

We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the reservation time may incur a fee of $50 per person. No-shows will be charged the full amount of the tasting menu.

Children aged 12 and above are welcome in our main dining room. For families with younger children, we recommend our private dining options where we can provide a more relaxed atmosphere. High chairs are available upon request.

Complimentary valet parking is available for all dinner guests. For lunch service, validated parking is available at the adjacent luxury parking garage. Both options are wheelchair accessible.

Our dress code is smart casual. Gentlemen are encouraged to wear collared shirts and dress pants. Ladies typically wear dresses or elegant separates. We respectfully request no shorts, athletic wear, or flip-flops.

With the exception of certified service animals, pets are not permitted inside the restaurant. However, we welcome well-behaved pets on our outdoor garden terrace, weather permitting.

Yes, we offer private dining rooms for events ranging from intimate gatherings to corporate receptions. Our events team can customize menus and experiences for groups of 8-40 guests. Please contact our events concierge for availability and pricing.
