Chef's Table Experience

An Intimate Culinary Journey

Experience the artistry of our kitchen up close with our exclusive Chef's Table. Watch as our culinary team crafts each course with meticulous precision, while our sommelier pairs each dish with rare vintages from our extensive collection. This immersive experience offers a behind-the-scenes glimpse into the passion and technique that defines L'Étoile.

Limited to six guests per evening, the Chef's Table includes a personalized tasting menu, wine pairings, and direct interaction with our Executive Chef and sommelier throughout the evening.
